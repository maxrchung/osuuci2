osu! UCI Fall LAN

osu! UCI is excited to host its first osu! LAN at UCI this fall quarter. Anyone is welcome to participate so long as they can attend the event in person. We will be creating two divisions of players to separate players of different ranking. This is a live BYOC(Bring Your Own Computer) event, so make sure to bring all your necessary gadgets and accessories.

Format

Day 1 - Saturday

  • Swiss
  • 6 rounds, one hour each
  • First to 4 wins
  • Top 8 advance to day 2

Day 2 - Sunday

  • Double elimination
  • 6 rounds, one hour each
  • Round of 8 is first to 5 wins
  • Semis, consolation, and finals are first to 6 wins

To accommodate varying ranks of players, we will be breaking up the tournament into two divisions: Master and Novice Divisions. The rank cutoffs will be decided by staff.

Rules

LAN rules and structure are subject to alterations by the administrators. Administrators have the final say in disputes.

  • Matches are in osu! standard with score as the win condition and head to head as team mode
  • !roll begins every match
  • Lower roll decides warm up map (any song <5 minutes, not in map pool)
  • Higher roll decides first map
  • Thereafter, game loser decides next map
  • Tiebreaker match decides last round
  • No Fail can be applied to preserve past scores
  • On first disconnect, the opponent decides to rematch or not
  • Second and further disconnects are counted as losses
  • Check in with your referee after your match to verify the end results
  • Missing your match counts as a loss; please show up a few minutes before your allotted time slot so you and the administrators can set up in time
  • Trolling, unsportsmanlike behavior, and other harassment will not be tolerated and can factor into your removal
