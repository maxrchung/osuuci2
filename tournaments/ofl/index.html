osu! UCI Fall LAN

osu! UCI will be holding it's very own LAN at the UCI Student Center in room [...]. Please note that this is a live BYOC(Bring Your Own Computer) event, so you will have to bring your computer and equipment. The event will span two days, November 7-8 from 11AM - 7PM.

Format

Day 1 Day 2
Swiss Stage Double Elimination
6 Rounds - 1 hour each Ro8 is 1 hour each, subsequent rounds are 1.5 hours each
Best of 7 Ro8: Best of 9
Top 8 advance to Day 2 Semis, Finals, Consolation: Best of 11

Divisions:

  • Novice Division and Master Division
  • Cutoff determined by staff
  • Prizing TBD

Rules:

  • All maps are free mod, with some having required mods.
  • Matches are in osu! standard with score as the win condition and head to head as team mode
  • !roll begins every match
  • Lower roll decides warm up map (any song <5 minutes, not in map pool)
  • Higher roll decides first map
  • Thereafter, game loser decides next map
  • Tiebreaker match decides last round
  • No Fail can be applied to preserve past scores
  • On first disconnect, the opponent decides to rematch or not
  • Second and further disconnects are counted as losses
  • Check in with your referee after your match to verify the end results
  • Missing your match counts as a loss; please show up a few minutes before your allotted time slot so you and the administrators can set up in time
  • Trolling, unsportsmanlike behavior, and other harassment will not be tolerated and can factor into your removal
  • League rules and structure are subject to alterations by the administrators
  • Administrators have the final say in disputes
