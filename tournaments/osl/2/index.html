Week 2 (July 18-19th)

Welcome everyone to week 2! This week we are expecting some entrants to drop before the matches start as they did not show up to week 1 or because of time commitment issues. Scheduling will be quite hectic as we try and scramble around to make matches for all of you guys or reschedule already planned matches in the case that does happen. Keep on the lookout for osu! messages and emails from us so we can communicate as effectively as possible.

  • Load your current beatmaps by pointing to your root osu! folder

  • Search for and download beatmaps that you are missing
  • Join our collection file with your collection file
  • Save the joined collection file as your new osu! collections
