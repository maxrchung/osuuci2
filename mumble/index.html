Mumble

We've set up a Mumble server for the club to replace our Skype group. With a dedicated VoIP server, it's a little easier to access and manage participants, though as downsides, Mumble isn't as popular and we can't keep chat logs. We'll try out Mumble for a bit, and if it turns out we like Skype more, we'll switch back. Anyone (genuinely) interested in osu! or the club is welcome to check us out, AFK, or hangout with us. Login info:

  • Port: 64738

Connect Instructions

To access our Mumble server (more properly known as Murmur), download and install the Mumble client and use that to connect with us. Up-to-date download links can be found on the official Mumble site for various operating systems.
